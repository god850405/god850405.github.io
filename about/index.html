個人簡介

我叫陳紫千，是來自高雄市桃園區建山部落的原住民布農族，後來因為父親工作因素而隨著家人定居雲林。從小就很常聽父親提起當時在山上的資源很缺乏、很困苦，父親的五位兄弟更因為生病沒錢治療，很早就相繼離世。因此，從小就立志長大後要努力打拼，讓自己能夠帶給家人更好的生活品質。
高中就讀於斗六高中，高二時曾擔任魔術社社長。是第一次任管理職並學習妥善地分配任務給幹部們。每次社課結束後，負責整合學員們的學習狀況並意見反饋給講師，進以促進整體的學習效率。大學就讀於淡江大學，開始一個人的生活並學會自我管理。在大學期間的專題製作擔任小組的組長，雖然部分組員程度有限，但為了團隊的凝聚力，我仍希望專題的作品可以藉由組員們的團隊合作，共同來完成。因此根據了組員的能力分配不同的任務，並協助程度不足的組員在做中學習，兼任他們的小老師。

My name is Zih Cian Chen. I am a Bunun, which is one of the aboriginals in Taiwan. It is located in Damahu Tribe in Kaohsiung. However, due to family reasons, I live in Mailiao , Yunlin now. When I was little, my father usually talks about his childhood in the tribe. It was a hard time because the tribe was lack of resources, and his brothers all passed away because they could not afford any medical treatment. Therefore, I set a goal to myself that I will do my best to bring my family a better life.
During my life in Douliu high school, I was the director of the Magic club. It was my first time to be responsible for a management position and helped me to learn about delegating the tasks to my cadre efficiently. After every single class, I collected student’s learning outcomes and feedback to the instructor. It contributed to improving our performance of the club by coordination.
Since I entered Tamkang University, I started living in Taipei alone and learned self-management. I was the team leader of the project at university. Although some member’s abilities were not enough to finish their tasks by themself. But I still insist to let every member participate in the project. Hence, I assign different tasks to members in accordance with their strengths. I also acted as a tutor to help some members to solve their problems. We improved together through learning by doing.
